# Interactive Webpages
Fun and useful interactive webpages


## [Sinkmoji](https://stinkmoji.cool/)  
An adorable face recognition experience combining face recognition and 3D modeling to celebrate Internet pop culture.

## [Semiconductor](https://semiconductor.withgoogle.com/)  
Conduct an AI orchestra through your browser using your webcam and machine learning.

## [Chrome music lab](https://musiclab.chromeexperiments.com/)  
Chrome Music Lab is a website that makes learning music more accessible through fun, hands-on interactive experiments.

## [Christmas experiments](https://christmasexperiments.com/)  
A digital advent calendar showcasing WebGL, canvas, and CSS3 interactive experiments by digital artists.

## [Piano Genie](https://magenta.github.io/magenta-js/music/demos/piano_genie.html)  
An intelligent musical interface created by the Google Magenta team that allows users to play a full 88-key piano in real time using only an 8-button controller or your computer keyboard.
