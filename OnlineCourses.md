# Online Courses
Some online courses that may be useful


## [100-Days-Of-ML-Code](https://github.com/Avik-Jain/100-Days-Of-ML-Code)  
A popular 100-day curriculum and repository covering the fundamentals of Machine Learning, including math, algorithms, and practical implementations.

## [WRF on AWS](https://github.com/aws-samples/aws-hpc-workshops/blob/master/README-WRF.rst)  
A tutorial workshop for running the Weather Research and Forecasting (WRF) model on AWS High Performance Computing (HPC) environments.

## [Time Series Visualization and Forecasting](https://www.digitalocean.com/community/tutorial_series/time-series-visualization-and-forecasting)  
A comprehensive tutorial series by DigitalOcean on time series data visualization and forecasting using Python (pandas, statsmodels, and ARIMA models).

## [Machine Learning Crash Course with TensorFlow APIs](https://developers.google.cn/machine-learning/crash-course/)
Google's fast-paced, practical introduction to machine learning, featuring a series of lessons with video lectures, real-world case studies, and hands-on coding exercises.
